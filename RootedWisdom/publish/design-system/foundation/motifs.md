## The library

Every motif listed below lives in the vault as an SVG asset. Filenames follow the pattern `[category]-[name].svg`. Used across the site, print materials, social graphics, and email — opacity ranges from 0.10 for background texture to 0.80 for focal motifs. Never animated.

## Usage guidance

<div class="fdn-spec">
Motif colors — rust: <em>#b5601a</em> · amber: <em>#c9882a</em> · blue: <em>#5b8aaa</em> · sage: <em>#7a8a5a</em> · gold: <em>#b87c1e</em> · bark: <em>#5a3218</em><br>
Background accents: <em>opacity 0.10–0.22</em> · Focal motifs: <em>opacity 0.50–0.80</em> · Never animate motifs<br>
Hero variants (Sun, Wave) use <em>currentColor</em> — they adopt whatever <em>color</em> property the parent element sets, allowing theme-aware deployment without hex edits.
</div>

**For print, social, or remixing.** SVGs are resolution-independent — open in Illustrator or Inkscape to adjust stroke weights if scaling above 10". To recolor for seasonal variations (deeper blues for winter, warmer ambers for autumn), find-and-replace the hex values in the SVG source.
